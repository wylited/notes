#+title: Angular Velocity

* Angular Velocity
Describes how fast a body rotates around a reference point, this can either be an orbit point or the bodies center of rotation for spin.

$$ omega = \frac{\delta theta}{\delta t} $$

with units $$ rad s^-1 $$
