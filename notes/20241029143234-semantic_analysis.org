#+title: Semantic Analysis
Semantic Analysis focuses on the meaning of words and sentences. It resolves [[id:813d3eab-f3bb-4411-b10f-557c9a936413][ambiguities]] and interprets context through processes like [[id:7da0c5ce-ca35-4f9a-932d-c470475f77d1][anaphora resolution]].
